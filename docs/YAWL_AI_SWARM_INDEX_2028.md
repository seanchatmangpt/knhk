## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Why 18 features? Why not 10 or 25?
**A:** These 18 represent distinct innovation categories that emerged from ideation. Each addresses a unique aspect of collective intelligence. More would be redundant; fewer would miss key opportunities.

### Q: How confident are we in the 2028 timeline?
**A:** Phase 1 (P0 features) is high confidence (75% probability). Phase 2 (P1 features) is medium confidence (60%). Phase 3 (P2 features) is lower confidence (40-50%) due to technology readiness uncertainty.

### Q: What if competitors build similar capabilities?
**A:** Our moat is YAWL workflow ontology + early market position + collective learning advantage (swarms get smarter over time). Even if competitors copy features, our swarms will have years of accumulated knowledge.

### Q: Which feature has the highest ROI?
**A:** SWARM-015 (Resilience) has fastest ROI (80% operational overhead reduction). SWARM-001 (Emergent Behavior) has highest strategic value (70% workflow maintenance cost reduction + platform lock-in).

### Q: What's the biggest risk?
**A:** Swarm collapse at scale (>500 agents). Mitigation: hierarchical organization (SWARM-006), economic incentives (SWARM-011), governance (SWARM-017). We'll know by end of Phase 1 if this is solvable.

### Q: Can we skip Phase 1 and go straight to advanced features?
**A:** No. P2-P3 features depend on P0-P1 infrastructure. E.g., Temporal Coordination (SWARM-007) requires Causal Inference (SWARM-013), which requires Swarm Learning (SWARM-005), which is P0. Skipping phases = guaranteed failure.

### Q: How does this relate to existing AI agent frameworks (AutoGPT, LangChain, etc.)?
**A:** Those are sequential task executors or tool frameworks. This is collective intelligence - agents that self-organize, learn from each other, and evolve. Different category entirely. See Executive Summary "Competitive Landscape" section.

### Q: What if AGI arrives before 2028?
**A:** If AGI arrives, swarm orchestration becomes even more valuable - coordinating multiple AGI agents safely. Our governance (SWARM-017) and ethics (SWARM-009) features become critical safety infrastructure.

### Q: How do we measure success?
**A:** Phase 1: Prove 100-agent swarms work (convergence <30s, 99.9% uptime). Phase 2: Enterprise deployments (10+ Fortune 500 customers). Phase 3: Market leadership (500+ deployments, $1B+ revenue).
