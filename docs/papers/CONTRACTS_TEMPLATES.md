    ├─ $500/month for 30-day period
    ├─ Priority response times (2-4 hours)
    ├─ Includes bug fixes and optimization
    └─ No minimum commitment

7. MARKETPLACE & REVENUE SHARING (Platinum Only)

7.1 Publication to ggen Marketplace
  Client authorizes Provider to publish the implementation to the ggen marketplace
  in return for revenue sharing as described below.

7.2 Revenue Sharing Terms
  ├─ Provider and Client split all license revenue 50/50
  ├─ Revenue includes: one-time purchases, licensing fees, and usage fees
  ├─ Payments made monthly within 15 days of month-end
  ├─ Minimum threshold: None (all revenue shared)
  └─ Term: 5 years, auto-renew unless either party opts out

7.3 Analytics & Transparency
  ├─ Provider provides Client with real-time analytics dashboard
  ├─ Dashboard shows: downloads, active users, deployments, revenue
  ├─ Quarterly business review calls to review performance
  └─ Client has full visibility into marketplace operations

7.4 Client Responsibilities
  ├─ Client grants Provider right to list implementation with Client's name/affiliation
  ├─ Client agrees to be identified as original researchers
  ├─ Client may request modifications to marketplace listing
  ├─ Client should promote implementation to maximize revenue

7.5 Termination of Marketplace Agreement
  ├─ Either party can terminate with 60 days notice
  ├─ Upon termination, implementation removed from marketplace

8. CONFIDENTIALITY

  ├─ Provider agrees to keep research confidential per Client's requirements
  ├─ Standard academic embargo: 1-2 years pre-publication

  ├─ Provider may publish a case study after implementation is public
  ├─ Provider may list Client as a reference (with permission)
  ├─ Upon publication, embargo restrictions lift

8.3 Non-Disclosure Agreement

  sign a separate NDA (see Section 9).

10. TERMINATION

    ├─ The other party materially breaches and fails to cure within 14 days
    ├─ The other party becomes insolvent or declares bankruptcy
    └─ The other party ceases business operations

10.2 Termination for Convenience
  Client may terminate this Agreement for convenience:
    ├─ Before Week 2: Refund 50% of deposit, no further obligations
    ├─ During Week 2-3: Provide work completed to date, no refund
    ├─ During Week 4: Deliver all work as if completion scheduled, pro-rata payment
    └─ After delivery: No refund, Service Agreement ends

10.3 Effect of Termination
  Upon termination:
    ├─ Client receives all code and documentation completed to date
    ├─ Provider ceases work and transfers access
    ├─ Support period is forfeited (unless paid in advance)
    ├─ Marketplace agreement terminates (if Platinum)
    └─ Both parties waive further claims

## 2. MARKETPLACE ADDENDUM (Platinum Package)

```
MARKETPLACE AGREEMENT ADDENDUM

This addendum is incorporated into the Service Agreement dated __________ between
[Client] and [Provider], and specifically applies to Platinum tier services.

---

1. MARKETPLACE PUBLICATION RIGHTS

1.1 Grant of Rights
  Client grants Provider the non-exclusive right to publish the implementation to
  the ggen marketplace, including:
    ├─ Source code
    ├─ Documentation
    ├─ Examples

    └─ Modifications and improvements

  Provider agrees to include proper attribution to Client/researchers throughout
  marketplace listing, code comments, and marketing materials.

1.3 Client Approval
  Provider will obtain Client approval of marketplace listing before publication.
  Client approval not to be unreasonably withheld.

---

2. REVENUE SHARING

2.1 Split Terms
  ├─ Client receives: 50% of all license revenue
  ├─ Provider receives: 50% of all license revenue
  ├─ Defined as "Gross Revenue": All fees, subscriptions, and usage charges

2.2 Exclusions from Revenue Sharing
  The following are NOT included in revenue sharing:
    ├─ Provider's operational costs (hosting, support staff, etc.)
    ├─ Taxes paid by Provider
    ├─ Payment processing fees (Stripe, etc.)
    └─ Marketplace admin fees

2.3 Payment Schedule
  ├─ Revenue calculated and paid monthly
  ├─ Payments made within 15 days of month-end
  ├─ Minimum payment: None (all revenue shared, even if <$100)
  ├─ Via: Wire transfer, ACH, check, or account credit
  └─ Detailed statement provided with each payment

2.4 Reporting
  ├─ Real-time dashboard access (updated daily)
  ├─ Monthly revenue report with transaction details
  ├─ Quarterly business review call
  ├─ Annual reconciliation and audit right

---

3. MARKETPLACE MANAGEMENT

3.1 Marketing & Optimization
  Provider is responsible for:
    ├─ Marketplace listing optimization (SEO, descriptions, tags)
    ├─ Marketing campaigns (email, social media, partnerships)
    ├─ Pricing strategy and updates
    ├─ User support and onboarding
    └─ Performance monitoring and improvements

3.2 Client Responsibilities
  Client should:
    ├─ Promote the implementation to colleagues and networks
    ├─ Provide testimonials or case studies
    ├─ Alert Provider to potential customers
    ├─ Provide feedback on marketplace performance
    └─ Notify Provider of academic or industry opportunities

3.3 Updates & Improvements
  Provider will periodically update implementation with:
    ├─ Performance improvements
    ├─ Bug fixes and security patches
    ├─ New features (based on user feedback)
    ├─ Documentation enhancements
    └─ New language support (if demand warrants)

---

4. TERM & TERMINATION

4.1 Initial Term
  This marketplace agreement runs for 5 years from the publication date.

4.2 Renewal
  After 5 years, agreement automatically renews for additional 1-year terms
  unless either party provides 60-day notice of non-renewal.

4.3 Termination by Either Party
  Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice. Upon termination:
    ├─ Implementation removed from marketplace within 30 days
    ├─ Client receives full source code and rights
    ├─ Revenue sharing continues for 12 months on residual income
    ├─ Client may distribute independently after termination

4.4 Effect of Termination
  ├─ New licenses cease, existing licenses honored
  ├─ Support transitions to Client or remains with Provider (separately negotiated)
  ├─ All marketplace branding and marketing materials updated
  └─ Final revenue settlement within 30 days of termination date

---

5. RESPONSIBILITIES & LIABILITY

5.1 Provider's Responsibilities
  ├─ Maintain code functionality and compatibility
  ├─ Provide security updates and patches
  ├─ Monitor marketplace performance
  ├─ Handle user support requests
  ├─ Ensure compliance with marketplace terms
  └─ Provide accurate revenue reporting

5.2 Client's Representations
  Client represents that:
    ├─ Client has the right to grant these rights
    ├─ The implementation does not infringe third-party IP
    ├─ The implementation is original work (derived from Client's research)
    └─ Client will not take conflicting actions

5.3 Indemnification
  Client indemnifies Provider against claims that the implementation infringes
  third-party intellectual property or violates any laws.

---

6. INDEPENDENT EVALUATION

6.1 No Partnership
  This agreement does not create a partnership, joint venture, or agency
  relationship. Each party is an independent contractor.

6.2 Separate Operations
  Provider operates the marketplace independently. Client has no say in
  Provider's business decisions, pricing, or operations (except for the
  specific listing and Client's implementation).

---

Signatures (same as main Service Agreement)
```

## 4. LEGAL CHECKLIST FOR CONTRACTS

```
Before Signing Any Contract:

TERMS TO NEGOTIATE:
  ☐ Scope is clearly defined and no ambiguity
  ☐ Timeline is realistic and achievable
  ☐ Payment schedule works for your cash flow
  ☐ Support terms are reasonable
  ☐ IP ownership is clearly assigned
  ☐ Liability limitations are acceptable
  ☐ Termination clauses are fair
  ☐ Confidentiality terms match your needs

RED FLAGS TO WATCH:
  ☐ Unlimited liability (push back)
  ☐ Unrealistic timeline (renegotiate)
  ☐ Indefinite payment terms (get deposit upfront)
  ☐ Client owns your tools/frameworks (protect your IP)
  ☐ Exclusive rights to your methodology (usually decline)
  ☐ Indemnification without limits (set boundaries)
  ☐ Ambiguous scope (too easy to become infinite)
  ☐ Personal liability (use LLC to protect personal assets)

PAYMENT SAFETY:
  ☐ 50% deposit before you start work
  ☐ Final 50% due upon delivery (not 30-60-90 days later)
  ☐ Clear definition of "delivery" and "acceptance"
  ☐ Right to pause work if payment is late
  ☐ Wire transfer or ACH preferred (avoid checks)

SCOPE PROTECTION:
  ☐ Scope document attached as Appendix A
  ☐ Change request process defined (with pricing)
  ☐ Clear definition of "out of scope" items
  ☐ Client approval required for scope changes
  ☐ Additional work billed at $150/hour

PROTECTION FOR YOU:
  ☐ Force majeure clause (act of God, etc.)
  ☐ Limitation of liability cap
  ☐ Right to use as reference/case study
  ☐ Right to write about methodology (after publication)
  ☐ Indemnification for Client's IP claims
  ☐ Severability clause (partial invalidity doesn't kill whole contract)
```
